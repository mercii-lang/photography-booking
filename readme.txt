📸 Photography Booking Platform
A smart and user-centric platform that connects clients with professional photographers and event venues, streamlining the process of booking, scheduling, and managing photography services and event spaces — all in one place.

🌟 Key Features
🔍 Discover photographers and venues based on location, availability, and services offered.

📆 Book photographers and venues directly through an intuitive calendar interface.

👥 User Roles:

Clients can browse, book, and review services.

Photographers can manage bookings, showcase portfolios, and accept/reject requests.

Venue Managers can list spaces, view booking requests, and manage availability.

💬 Messaging System for real-time communication between clients and service providers.

📁 Portfolio Integration for photographers to display their work.

📍 Location-Based Search for relevant results.
